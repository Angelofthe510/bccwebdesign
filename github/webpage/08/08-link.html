Star Trek GNDN

In the Official Star Trek Universe, GNDN is an acronym for Goes Nowhere, Does Nothing and is part of the numerical designation of all the Jeffries Tubes on any starship.

Matt Jeffries

Walter Mathew Jeffries was the art director wo worked with Gene Roddenberry to create the USS Enterprise and the look and feel of the starship's interiors. You can read more here.

The Jeffries Tube

Almost as famous as the ship itself is the access crawlway where Scotty was often seen fixing the ship. It was referred to off-screen as the "Jefferies Tube."
