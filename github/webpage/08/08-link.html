Matt Jeffries

The Designer

Walter Mathew Jeffries was the art director wo worked with Gene Roddenberry to create the USS Enterprise and the look and feel of the starship's interiors. You can read more here.

A Place in History

The original filmming model of the Enterprise is now in the Smithsonian Air and Space Museum. It has undergone at least three complete re-constructions.
